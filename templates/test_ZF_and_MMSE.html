Experiment Instructions

  1. Enter a binary data string (e.g., "1011", 2-8 bits recommended).
  2. Click "Simulate Channel" to pass the signal through a noisy, fading channel.
  3. Select an equalization technique (ZF or MMSE) to see the results.
  4. The signal flow shows the process for your selected technique, and the table below compares both.

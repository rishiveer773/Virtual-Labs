Wireless Communication Equalizer

Interactive Simulation of ZF and MMSE Equalization Techniques

Experiment Instructions

  1. Enter a binary data string (e.g., "1011", 2-8 bits recommended) to simulate transmission.
  2. Click "Simulate Channel" to pass the signal through a noisy, fading channel.
  3. Choose an equalization technique (ZF or MMSE) to recover the original signal.
  4. Observe the Bit Error Rate (BER) and compare the original vs. recovered data.
Please enter only 0s and 1s (2-8 bits).
